If you like to walk and see airplanes, I recommend you go to Coyote Point in San Mateo or the little trail by the shore in Burlingame. Once you get to Bayfront Park in Millbrae, you will see airplanes up close! At Coyote Point , you can get a very nice view and occasionally airplanes fly right above your head, which, I think, is cool. You can see San Francisco and the East Bay from there if the weather is nice.
