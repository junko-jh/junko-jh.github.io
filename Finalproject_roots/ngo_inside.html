nagoya

Although Nagoya is the third largest city in Japan,
it is often forgotten and ignored. Sad!

Things to Do

•    •    •

Nagoya airport is quite small, but there are many restaurants and shops available. What I usually do right before leaving Japan is to eat Soba at Sarrasin with my family. Then if we have time, we walk around. Apparently, after I go through security, my family goes to Sky Deck, which is an outsite terrace-like place at the top level of the airport building, and wait until my plane takes off.
